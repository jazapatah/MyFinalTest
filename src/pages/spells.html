Accio (Summoning Charm)

Summons an object towards the caster. It is able to summon objects in direct line of sight of the caster, as well as things out of view, by calling the object aloud after the incantation (unless the spell is casted nonverbally). This spell needs thought behind it, and the object must be clear in the casters mind before trying to summon it. (see wand movement)

Aguamenti (Water-Making Spell)

Produces a clean, drinkable jet of water from the wand tip. (see wand movement)

Alarte Ascendere

Shoots the target high into the air.

Alohomora (Unlocking Charm)

Unlocks doors and other locked objects. It can also unlock doors that have been sealed with a Locking Spell, although it is possible to bewitch doors to become unaffected by this spell. (see wand movement)

Appare Vestigium (Tracking Spell)

Reveals traces of magic, including footprints and track marks.

Apparition

Magically transports the caster to another location instantaneously. The destination is one that the primary user has been to or seen in some fashion previously. Can be used to apparate multiple people at once if holding each other. No incantation required.

Arresto Momentum (Slowing Charm)

Decreases the velocity of a moving target. Can be used on multiple targets, as well as on the caster themselves.

Ascendio

Lifts the caster high into the air. The charm also works underwater, propelling the caster above the surface. (see wand movement)

Avada Kedavra (Killing Curse)

Causes instantaneous death. It is accompanied by a flash of green light and a rushing noise. There is no known Counter-curse that can protect the victim from dying, except for a loving sacrifice. It is one of the three Unforgivable Curses. (see wand movement)

Avenseguim

Was the incantation of a charm that turned a target object into a tracking device.

Bombarda Maxima

Creates a large explosion capable of removing entire walls. A more advanced and more powerful form of the Exploding Charm.

Bridge-conjuring spell

Conjure a bridge from thin air.

Capacious extremis (Extension Charm)

Expands the internal dimensions of an object without affecting the external dimensions, enhancing its capacity, and rendering its contents lighter.

Circumrota

Rotates objects.

Colloportus (Locking Spell)

Locks doors and all things that can be locked. It is the counter-charm to the Unlocking Spell. (see wand movement)

Confringo (Blasting Curse)

Produces a fiery explosion. (see wand movement)

Confundo (Confundus Charm)

Causes the victim to become confused and befuddled. (see wand movement)

Crucio (Cruciatus Curse)

Inflicts intense pain on the recipient of the curse. This curse does not physically harm the victim, but may in extreme cases drive them insane. The pain is described as having hot knives being driven into the victim. It cannot be cast successfully by a person who is doing so out of pure spite or anger; one must feel a true desire to cause the victim pain. If one casts this spell, he or she will receive a life sentence in Azkaban prison for it, as with the other two Unforgivable Curses.

Diffindo (Severing Charm)

Used to precisely cut or tear objects. (see wand movement)

Diminuendo

Forces the target to shrink. (see wand movement)

Engorgio (Engorgement Charm)

FCauses the target to swell in physical size. Its counter-charm is the Shrinking Charm. (see wand movement)

Erecto

Used to erect a tent or other upright structure.

Everte Statum

Throws the victim backward, similarly to being thrown.

Expecto Patronum (Patronus Charm)

His charm is a highly powerful and advanced protective spell which will conjure a spirit guardian of their positive emotions to defend against dark creatures; it can also send messages to other witches or wizards. The Patronus takes the form of an animal, unique to each person who casts it. The form of a Patronus can change when one has undergone a period of heightened emotion. (see wand movement)

Expelliarmus (Disarming Charm)

Forces whatever an opponent is holding to fly out of their hand. (see wand movement)

Expulso (Expulso Curse)

Provokes an explosion, unique in that it uses pressure to do so as opposed to heat.

Fianto Duri

A defensive charm which, based on the etymology, strengthens shield spells, and perhaps objects in general, in a similar way to Duro.

Finite Incantatem (General Counter-Spell)

Terminates all spell effects in the vicinity of the caster. (see wand movement)

Geminio (Doubling Charm)

Duplicates the target. When used to duplicate objects indefinitely on purpose, is known as the Gemino Curse.

Immobulus (Freezing Charm)

Immobilises and stops the actions of the target. It works both on living and inanimate things. (see wand movement)

Imperio (Imperius Curse)

Places the victim completely under the caster's control. The victim is put into a trance-like state, and becomes very suggestible to the commands of the caster. However, those who are strong willed may learn to resist it. One of the three "Unforgivable Curses," the use of this curse on another human results in capital punishment or life sentence in Azkaban.

Incarcerous (Incarcerous Spell)

Ties up the target with ropes conjured from thin air. (see wand movement)

Incendio (Fire-Making Spell)

Produces fire. (see wand movement)

Lacarnum Inflamari

Ignites cloaks. (see wand movement)

Legilimens (Legilimency Spell)

Allows the caster to delve into the mind of the victim, allowing the caster to see the memories, thoughts, and emotions of the victim. (see wand movement)

Lumos (Wand-Lighting Charm)

Illuminates the tip of the caster's wand, allowing the caster to see in the dark. (see wand movement)

Lumos Solem (Lumos Solem Spell)

Produce a blinding flash of sunlight. It is a variant of the Wand-Lighting Charm.

Nebulus

Creates fog from the tip of the wand. (see wand movement)

Oculus Reparo

Mends eyeglasses.

Obliviate (Memory Charm)

Erases specific memories. (see wand movement)

Oppugno (Oppugno Jinx)

Causes targeted objects to attack a victim. It is particularly effective in conjunction with the Bird-Conjuring Charm.

Papyrus Reparo

Mends torn pieces of paper.

Periculum

Produces a burst of red sparks.

Petrificus Totalus (Full Body-Bind Curse)

Used to temporarily bind the victim's body in a position much like that of a soldier at attention; the victim will usually fall to the ground. (see wand movement)

Piertotum Locomotor

Brings animates inanimate targets.

Protego (Shield Charm)

Invisible shield that reflects spells and blocks physical entities. (see wand movement)

Protego Diabolica

Conjures a protective ring of black fire around the caster that only affects their enemies.

Reducto (Reductor Curse)

Breaks objects. In stronger usages, disintegrates them. (see wand movement)

Reverte

Returns objects to their original positions or states.

Relashio (Revulsion Jinx)

Forces the target to release its grip on whatever it is holding. (see wand movement)

Reparo (Mending Charm)

Seamlessly repairs broken objects. (see wand movement)

Revelio (Revelio Charm)

Reveals secrets about a person or object. (see wand movement)

Rictusempra (Tickling Charm)

Tickles the target until they become weak with laughter. (see wand movement)

Riddikulus (Boggart Banishing Spell)

A spell used when fighting a Boggart, "Riddikulus" forces the Boggart to take the appearance of an object the caster is focusing on. Best results can be achieved if the caster is focusing on something humorous, with the desire that laughter will weaken the Boggart.

Scorching Spell

Produces dancing flames which presumably scorch the opponent.

Sectumsempra

Lacerates the target, as if they have been "slashed by a sword." Subsequently, the target can easily bleed to death from the wounds.

Serpensortia (Snake Summons Spell)

Conjures a serpent from the caster's wand. (see wand movement)

Stupefy (Stunning Spell)

Stuns the target, rendering them unconscious. (see wand movement)

Surgito

Counter-charm that can be used to remove enchantments.

Vera Verto

Turn animals to water goblets. (see wand movement)

Vulnera Sanentur

Healing spell that slows blood flow, clears residue, and knits wounds. (see wand movement)

Wingardium Leviosa (Levitation Charm)

Makes objects fly, or levitate. (see wand movement)
